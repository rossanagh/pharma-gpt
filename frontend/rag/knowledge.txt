[ESC 2024] Hypertension (European Society of Cardiology / European Society of Hypertension):
- BP targets typically <140/90 mmHg for most; consider <130/80 mmHg if tolerated in high-risk.
- Assess overall CV risk, comorbidities, and organ damage when choosing therapy.

[EASD/ADA 2024] Type 2 Diabetes:
- For T2DM with CKD/HF/ASCVD, consider SGLT2 inhibitor and/or GLP-1 RA regardless of baseline HbA1c, individualized.
- Prioritize cardio-renal protection and hypoglycemia avoidance.

[EMA SmPC] Off‑label warning:
- If a requested use is off‑label, say it explicitly and prefer guideline-supported alternatives.
- Always include key contraindications and clinically relevant interactions when discussing medicines.

[ECDC] Antimicrobial stewardship (core principles):
- Use antibiotics only when bacterial infection is likely; follow local resistance patterns.
- Narrow spectrum when possible; reassess at 48–72h (“review and revise”); document indication/duration.

[WHO Europe] Respiratory infections (general):
- Avoid antibiotics for uncomplicated viral URTI/acute bronchitis; focus on supportive care and safety-net advice.
- Escalate care for red flags: hypoxia, respiratory distress, sepsis, altered mental status, severe dehydration.

[ESC] Secondary prevention (ASCVD – general):
- High‑intensity lipid lowering and lifestyle interventions are central; assess blood pressure, diabetes, smoking, and adherence.
- Consider renal function and drug–drug interactions (e.g., statins, anticoagulants).

[EMA SmPC] Anticoagulants (general safety):
- Always check renal function (CrCl/eGFR) and interacting drugs (strong CYP/P‑gp inhibitors/inducers where applicable).
- Bleeding risk mitigation: review antiplatelets/NSAIDs, gastroprotection where appropriate, and patient counselling.

[MS RO] (Romania) Clinical governance:
- Follow Romanian national protocols/orders where applicable; document clinical reasoning and informed consent.
- For notifiable diseases and outbreak measures, follow national public health guidance aligned with ECDC/WHO.

[CNAS] (Romania) Reimbursement / prescribing (general):
- Prescribing and reimbursement rules depend on diagnosis, specialty, and therapeutic line; verify the latest CNAS norms.
- When relevant, advise to check current national reimbursement criteria and electronic prescription requirements.

[ECDC] Vaccination / prevention (general):
- Prioritize vaccination for high‑risk groups; ensure up‑to‑date status per national schedules.
- For exposure management and outbreak response, align with ECDC risk assessments and local public health.

[EMA SmPC] Pregnancy / lactation:
- If pregnancy or breastfeeding is possible, check SmPC sections for pregnancy/lactation and contraindications.
- Prefer agents with established safety data; avoid teratogenic medicines and flag need for specialist advice.

[ESC] Hypertension treatment (stepwise, general):
- Lifestyle + risk reduction are foundational; start pharmacotherapy based on BP level + total CV risk and comorbidities.
- Ensure correct measurement, assess secondary causes when severe/resistant, and monitor adherence.

[EASD/ADA] Diabetes (cardio‑renal focus, general):
- For CKD/HF/ASCVD, prioritize therapies with proven outcomes; adjust doses by renal function where relevant.
- Avoid hypoglycemia in older/frail; individualize glycemic targets.
